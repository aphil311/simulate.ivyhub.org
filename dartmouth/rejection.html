Dear John,

After a thoughtful consideration of your candidacy, the Admissions Committee has denied your application to join our Class of 2024. By informing you of our final decision at this time, we hope you will consider other options for your post-secondary education.

I know this was not the outcome you hoped to receive. While many candidates for admission to Dartmouth could thrive academically and personally at the College, sheer numbers required us to make difficult decisions like this one. Regrettably, the small size of our first-year class prevented us from selecting more candidates from the record pool of talented and promising applicants who submitted an application this year.

Thank you for the compliment of your strong interest in Dartmouth, and please accept our best wishes for a successful college experience.

Sincerely,


Lee A. Coffin
Dean of Admission and Financial Aid
